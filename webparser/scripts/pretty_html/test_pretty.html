Theory of Computation

Credit: 3 hours.

Finite automata and regular languages; pushdown automata and context-free languages; Turing machines and recursively enumerable sets; computability and the halting problem; undecidable problems.

Prerequisite: CS 173 or MATH 213; CS 225.

Students must register for one lecture and one discussion section.

This course satisfies the General Education Criteria in Spring 2014 for a
UIUC: Quant Reasoning II course
